Engineering Student | University of British Columbia.

Who am I? The classic personal profile question I filled out years ago.

To my peers, I'm an electrical engineering student who won't shut up about my biomedical specialization in the program.

To my close friends, I'm an absolute nerd who can talk for hours about cybersecurity, the seemingly desolate internet ecosystem of today, and exploring the small internet.

To members of my community, I'm that person who always has a sketchbook out, taking visual notes to absorb information in discussions and meetings.

As for strangers, most believe that I'm an English major, perhaps because I always carry a book around with me.

To myself, I am all of the above. A blend of methodical troubleshooting and creative, outside-of-the-box solutions. As always, stay curious. There's no such thing as a stupid question, so keep asking them.
